FAQ'S

How does Seva Charities help Akshaya Patra?

We hold social media campaigns and events open to the UT community where all proceeds are donated to Akshaya Patra. During the 2021-2022 school year, we were able to raise close to $12,000, which was enough to feed 600 kids for a year!

What does it take to become a member?

Just sign up using our membership form, pay dues ($40 for the entire year!), and join our group me.

How often do we meet?

Seva Charities holds meetings every other Monday at 6:30 pm. We also will have various socials, fundraising events, and philanthropic events each month for our members! These meetings and events are not mandatory, but are highly encouraged.

What does the time commitment look like and how can I get more involved?

You can be involved as you choose! We have many opportunities for our members to be engaged socially and philanthropically through socials, philanthropy events, and volunteering Seva Events at fundraising events. We also have Seva Circles, where we match our members to smaller groups of Seva members that can get to know each other throughout the semester.

Where can I find more information about all our events?

Our general meetings will be the best place to learn about all upcoming events and opportunities, and our groupme will have all of the information as well. You can also subscribe to our member calendar and follow us on instagram to learn more!
