Family is an integral part of Seva, as it embodies the values of love, compassion, and service. We practice the act of selfless service, which represents the foundation of Seva. Our family and friends are shaped by both the history and future of Seva. Our work for Akshaya Patra would never be possible without the family within Seva Charities. Whether it be through amazing socials, competitive events, local volunteering work, or our circles, our members grow to form lifelong friendships while making a difference around the world.
